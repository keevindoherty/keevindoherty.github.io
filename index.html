Kevin Doherty

I'm an undergraduate student at Stevens Institute of Technology studying Electrical Engineering and minoring in Computer Science. I currently research autonomous robotics under Prof. Brendan Englot in the Robust Field Autonomy Lab, where my research focus has been broadly at the intersection of machine learning and robotics, with specific applications in underwater mapping and exploration.

Most recently, I was a summer research intern at MIT Lincoln Laboratory where I worked on problems in robot mapping.
